Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 June 2025

1. Introduction

Chat Copier (“the Extension”, “we”) is a Chrome side-panel tool that lets you copy conversations from ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity, optionally summarise them with Google Gemini, and paste them back into any active tab. We respect your privacy: the Extension runs entirely in your browser and never transmits personal or sensitive data to us or to third parties.

2. What information do we handle?

DataSource / StoragePurposeRetention
Conversation text you copyExtracted from the visible page; stored only in side-panel preview and (optionally) your clipboardAllows copy → preview → paste workflowCleared when you press Clear, close the panel, or quit Chrome

Remembered AI-tab IDs & URLsChrome tabs API; stored in chrome.storage.localLets you paste into the correct AI tab laterRemoved automatically when the tab closes or when you uninstall the Extension

3. How do we use the information?

  • Provide the copy / summarise / paste features.
  • Persist your Gemini settings.
  • Remember which ChatGPT / Claude / Perplexity tab to target.

4. Sharing of information

We share no data with any server or third-party service. If you use the Gemini feature, your browser sends the conversation text to Google’s Gemini API over HTTPS; that request is subject to Google’s Privacy Policy.

5. Security

  • All Gemini traffic uses HTTPS (TLS 1.2+).
  • No remote code is executed; all injected scripts are bundled in the extension.
  • Data stored via chrome.storage.* is sandboxed by Chrome; sync data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

6. Your choices

  • Press Clear to remove copied text.
  • Edit or delete your Gemini key in the in-panel Settings drawer.
  • Disable or remove the Extension at any time via chrome://extensions.

7. Changes

We’ll update this page and the Chrome Web Store listing before any change that affects how data is handled.
