Saksham Gupta

I am an undergraduate final year student majoring in Electrical & Electronics Engineering and Physics from BITS Pilani, Pilani Campus. I am currently working under Prof. Ravi Kiran S. on Instance based Diffusion models at the Part - level.

I am also presently mentored by Prof. Pratik Narang at BITS, where I am working on Deepfake Detection in Deep Learning models. I was also mentored by Prof. Vinay Chamola at BITS under whom I worked on models involving Classification using Diffusion models on Automonous Driving and Medical Imaging domain.

My interests lie in mitigating the risks of Modern Deep Learning. Through this motivation, I work in fairness, model-based privacy, and causal inference. I am also interested in information-theoretic foundations of Machine Learning, and spend time studying allied fields such as Grokking and Unlearning.
